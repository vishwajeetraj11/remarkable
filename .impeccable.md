## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users are reMarkable tablet owners looking for printable/downloadable puzzle and planning content.
- Secondary users are parents/teachers generating kid-friendly learning worksheets.
- Core job-to-be-done: quickly generate clean, e-ink-friendly PDFs (puzzles, templates, kids activities) with minimal setup.

### Brand Personality
- Practical, trustworthy, and educational.
- Tone should feel simple, calm, and utility-first rather than flashy.
- Emotional goal: users feel confident they can generate useful content fast.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Clear, low-noise UI optimized for readability and utility.
- Light-first neutral palette with good contrast; dark mode support remains functional but not dominant.
- Anti-reference: avoid trendy/glossy AI visuals that reduce clarity for a tool workflow.

### Design Principles
1. Optimize for completion speed: users should get to a generated PDF in as few steps as possible.
2. Keep interaction semantics explicit and accessible (keyboard, labels, focus visibility).
3. Favor tokenized, consistent styling over one-off visual exceptions.
4. Maintain responsive layouts that preserve controls and readability on small screens.
5. Prioritize meaningful hierarchy over decorative UI patterns.
